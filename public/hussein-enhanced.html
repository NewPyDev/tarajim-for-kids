Hussein ibn Ali was born in 626 CE as the younger son of Ali ibn Abi Talib and Fatimah, making him the Prophet's cherished grandson. Like his brother Hassan, he was deeply loved by the Prophet, who called both of them 'the leaders of the youth of Paradise.'
