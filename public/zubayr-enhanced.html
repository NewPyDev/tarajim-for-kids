Az-Zubayr ibn Al-Awwam was the Prophet's cousin and one of the ten companions promised Paradise. He was famous for his exceptional courage and was considered one of the best swordsmen of his time. He accepted Islam when he was very young - only 15 years old - and immediately faced persecution from his uncle who would wrap him in a mat and light a fire under it, trying to make him give up his faith. But young Zubayr never wavered. He was the first person to draw his sword in defense of Islam and participated in all the major battles.
