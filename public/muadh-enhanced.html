Mu'adh ibn Jabal

The Young Scholar

⚖️

Mu'adh ibn Jabal was one of the youngest companions but also one of the most knowledgeable in Islamic law. The Prophet said 'The most knowledgeable of my Ummah in matters of halal and haram is Mu'adh ibn Jabal.' Despite his young age, the Prophet trusted him so much that he sent him to Yemen as a governor and teacher. Before he left, the Prophet gave him famous advice about how to judge between people, telling him to use the Quran first, then the Sunnah, then his own reasoning. This became a foundation for Islamic jurisprudence.

💡 Lessons We Learn

Young people can achieve great knowledge and wisdom
True knowledge leads to humility and fear of Allah
Teaching others is one of the best ways to serve Islam
Understanding halal and haram is crucial for every Muslim

"Learn knowledge, for learning it for Allah's sake is fear of Him, seeking it is worship, discussing it is glorification of Allah."

- Mu'adh ibn Jabal
