Khalid ibn Al-Walid was one of the greatest military strategists in history, earning the title 'Sayf Allah al-Maslul' (The Drawn Sword of Allah) from Prophet Muhammad. Initially, he was one of Islam's fiercest enemies and led the cavalry that caused significant damage to the Muslim army at the Battle of Uhud. However, his conversion to Islam marked a turning point in Islamic military history. Once he embraced Islam, he never lost a single battle in over 100 engagements.
